Our Teachers

At Sunshine Kindergarten, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly qualified, passionate teachers dedicated to nurturing young minds. Each teacher brings a unique set of skills, qualifications, and a deep commitment to fostering creativity, learning, and emotional growth in every child.

Ms. Emily Johnson – Early Childhood Educator

Qualification: Bachelor's Degree in Early Childhood Education, Certified Montessori Educator

Experience: 8 years in early childhood education

Specialization: Montessori learning methods, social and emotional development

Fun Fact: Ms. Emily loves integrating nature-based learning into her classroom and often incorporates outdoor exploration to teach science and sensory skills.

Mr. Ahmed Khalid – Language and Literacy Specialist

Qualification: Master’s Degree in Education with a focus on Early Literacy

Experience: 10 years of teaching young learners

Specialization: Early language acquisition, storytelling, and phonics

Fun Fact: Mr. Ahmed is passionate about storytelling and often uses puppet shows and interactive reading sessions to inspire a love for reading in his students.

Ms. Sarah Green – Creative Arts and Craft Instructor

Qualification: Bachelor's Degree in Art Education, Diploma in Early Childhood Development

Experience: 6 years of teaching arts and crafts in kindergartens

Specialization: Creative expression through arts, sensory play, and fine motor skill development

Fun Fact: Ms. Sarah is a professional painter and loves helping children express themselves through painting, drawing, and craft projects.

Ms. Fatima Al-Rahman – Math and Problem-Solving Expert

Qualification: Bachelor’s Degree in Early Childhood Education, Specialization in Early Mathematics

Experience: 7 years of teaching math to young learners

Specialization: Introducing math through fun games, puzzles, and hands-on activities

Fun Fact: Ms. Fatima is known for turning everyday objects into math tools, making learning math an exciting and interactive experience for kids.

Mr. Lucas White – Physical Education and Outdoor Activities Coordinator

Qualification: Diploma in Physical Education, Certification in Child Fitness and Well-being

Experience: 5 years of teaching physical education in kindergarten settings

Specialization: Gross motor skill development, teamwork, and healthy habits

Fun Fact: Mr. Lucas loves to organize obstacle courses and outdoor games to help kids stay active while developing their coordination and team spirit.
